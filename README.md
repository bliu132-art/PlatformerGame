Platformer Game

A small platformer game made with Phaser 3 and Tiled.

The player must jump across platforms, avoid hazards, collect diamonds, and reach the goal flag at the end of the level.

Controls
A / Left Arrow = Move Left
D / Right Arrow = Move Right
SPACE = Jump
R = Restart after finishing level
Features
Tilemap level made in Tiled
Hazard system (spikes, water, crystal hazards)
HP system
Coin / diamond collection system
Particle effects
Enemy movement system
Camera follow system
Goal flag and mission complete screen
Sound effects
HUD system
Optional Elements Implemented
1. Hazard Tiles

Added spike traps, crystal hazards, and water hazards that damage the player.

Estimated Points: 5

2. HP System

Player has 3 HP instead of instantly dying from every hazard.

Estimated Points: 5

3. Enemy AI

Enemies patrol platforms and reverse direction at edges without falling off.

Estimated Points: 5

4. Collectibles

Diamonds can be collected and tracked with a coin counter.

Estimated Points: 3

5. Particle Effects

Added collect particle effects and water bubble particle effects.

Estimated Points: 3

6. Camera Follow

Camera follows the player throughout the level.

Estimated Points: 2

Puzzle / Gameplay Notes
Some diamonds are placed near hazards and require careful jumps.
The industrial area contains tighter platforming sections.
Enemies can block narrow platforms, so timing jumps is important.
